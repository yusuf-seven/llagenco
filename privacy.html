Privacy & Data Policy

Data as a Trust

At LLAGOX, we recognize that your personal data is a trust. We collect only what is strictly necessary for operational fulfillment: your name, shipping address, and WhatsApp number. Your privacy is prioritized and protected in strict accordance with Islamic ethical standards and European data regulations.

No Data Brokering

We categorically state that we do not, and will never, sell, trade, or transfer your customer data to third-party brokers or external marketing agencies. Your information remains entirely within our secure, closed operational ecosystem.

Analytics & Tracking

To measure the operational efficiency of our own advertising, we utilize minimal, anonymized tracking pixels (such as Meta and Google). This is executed in full compliance with GDPR regulations. You maintain total control and may opt out of these fundamental analytics at any time via your browser settings or ad-blocking extensions.

Data Deletion & Your Rights

You have the absolute right to request the immediate deletion of your data post-fulfillment. To have your information purged from our secure records, please submit a formal request to support@llagox.com, and we will process it without delay.
